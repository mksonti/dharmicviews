# The Essence of Vedic Wisdom

The Vedic tradition is one of the oldest continuous knowledge systems in the world. It encompasses a vast body of literature, including the Vedas, Upanishads, Puranas, and Itihasas.

## Core Concepts

1. **Dharma**: The principle of cosmic order and righteous living.
2. **Karma**: The law of cause and effect.
3. **Moksha**: Liberation from the cycle of birth and death.
